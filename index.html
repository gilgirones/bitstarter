I Was There It aims to be the medium where anyone with a story to tell about a place the world can share. In this way, people will have access to inside information, and estimate much more the place you are in, you want to visit or meet.

With this application you can tell us your experience in a historic moment, share your photos, and find other people who lived the same moment, in the same place in the world, at the same time as you. Been in Woodstock? Saw the fall of the Berlin wall? Please, tell us about it, the world must know
